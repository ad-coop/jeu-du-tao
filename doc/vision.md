# Vision

The _Jeu du Tao_ helps players achieve their goals by harnessing collective
intelligence, fostering connection and mutual support.

This is an online adaptation of the _Jeu du Tao_. The application must preserve
the essence of the physical game: cooperation, kindness, active listening,
freedom of expression, and clarity. Players play together, not against each
other — your only opponent is yourself.

## Gameplay

Players are distributed, each on their own device. The application should
deliver the same quality of experience as a physical game session, despite the
distance.

A Guardian initiates a game and invites other players to join. Together, they
use collective intelligence to help each other progress on their personal quest.

## Play modes

The application will support two play modes:

1. **Guardian-led** — The Guardian leads the game. The application provides
   structural support (board, turns, timers), as a physical game set would.
2. **AI-assisted** — An Artificial Intelligence provides structure and feedback
   to players throughout the game. The Guardian supervises the session and can
   override or complement the AI's guidance.
